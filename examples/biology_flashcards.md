Q2: What is a cell?
A2: The basic structural and functional unit of all known living organisms.

Q3: What is photosynthesis?
A3: The process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy.

Q4: What is respiration?
A4: The process by which organisms convert food into energy.

Q5: What is a gene?
A5: A unit of heredity that is transferred from a parent to offspring.

Q6: What is an ecosystem?
A6: A community of living organisms and their physical environment.

Q7: What is evolution?
A7: The process of change in the heritable characteristics of biological populations over successive generations.

Q8: What is a food chain?
A8: A series of organisms each dependent on the next as a source of food.

Q9: What is a chromosome?
A9: A structure in the cell that contains DNA.

Q10: What is a protein?
